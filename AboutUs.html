OUR MISSION:

On one hand, there are peoples who live their live lavishly. They aren't only enjoying the joy of essentials but also have what they want and desire. On other hand,there are peoples who don't have food to eat, enough clothing and heath care. Being poor means deprived economically, politically and socially. Our mission is to help these peoples and want to please Allah (SWT) by helping his fellowmen. Our mission is to remove poverty from Pakistan and slowly from the whole world. Because,

Caring for the poor and needy people and helping them is the noble endeavor
