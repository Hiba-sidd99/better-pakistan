Ramdan Distribution

Better Pakistan successfully completed its Ramadan campaign which involved Iftar drive that was conducted at hyderabad and team also reflected the value of caring that included distributing Eid clothing to the street children and to the children of underprivileged families. Some of the clothes were also distributed among the children in Luminary learning circle foundation school.Throughout the month of Ramadan our objective was to engage ourselves in helping the maximum number of needy families by providing them basic food items and Allahmadullillah we succedeed in our mission .Thanks to the overwhelming participation from everyone.


Distibution At Tharparkar & Awareness Program

Team Better Pakistan visited primary schools located in #Malanhor village in #tharparkar district where almost 150 children are enrolled.The team conducted small sessions about health and safety hazards in schools to make them aware of cleanliness and harmful diseases and also distributed pencil boxes,colouring books,notebooks and colors among the children of school.The children were so happy and excited to receive the distributed items;the smiles and happiness on their face was clearly seen
